Chickpea Curry with Rice

I realy like this one

A fast way to make a deliciously traditional Indian curry dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp cilantro
  • 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Finely chop the onion. Mince the garlic
  3. Heat a drizzle of vegetable oil in a pan. Add the garlic, onion & spices (curry, cumin & ginger powders). Cook, stirring, for 4 minutes.
  4. Add the tomatoes, coconut milk & 1/4 cup of water (per serving) to the pan. Stir. When it comes to a boil, add the chickpeas. Lower the heat, cover & simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve saucy chickpea curry over the rice. Garnish with cilantro. Enjoy!
