Chickpea Curry with Rice

I realy like this one

A fast way to make a deliciously traditional Indian curry dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp cilantro
  • 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
